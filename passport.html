DOCUMENTS REQUIRED

The following documents are required for passport application:

Aadhar Card

  • Original Aadhar card required
  • Clear photocopy (front and back)
  • Must be linked with mobile number

PAN Card

  • Original PAN card required
  • Clear photocopy
  • Name must match with other documents

Power Bill

  • Electricity bill of current residence
  • Not older than 3 months
  • Must show applicant's name and address

Leaving Certificate

  • Original school leaving certificate
  • For minors and first-time applicants
  • Must be attested if required

Birth Certificate

  • Original birth certificate
  • Issued by municipal authority
  • Required for minors and first-time applicants
