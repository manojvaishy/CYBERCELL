DOCUMENTS REQUIRED

The following documents are required for driving license application:

Voter ID or PAN Card

  • Proof of identity and date of birth
  • Original document required
  • Clear photocopy (front and back)
ID Card Example

Power Bill

  • Proof of current residential address
  • Not older than 3 months
  • Must show applicant's name and address
Utility Bill Example

Passport Size Photo

  • Recent color photograph
  • White background
  • 35mm x 45mm size
  • 2 copies required
Photo Example

Signature

  • Signature specimen on white paper
  • Must match signature on ID documents
  • Black or blue ink
