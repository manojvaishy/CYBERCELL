DOCUMENTS REQUIRED

The following documents are required for government certificate applications:

Aadhar Card

  • Mandatory identity proof
  • Original and photocopy required
  • Must be linked with mobile number

Ration Card

  • Proof of family composition
  • Original required for verification
  • Photocopy of front and back

Power Bill

  • Proof of current residence
  • Not older than 3 months
  • Must show applicant's name and address

Bank Passbook

  • First page with account details
  • Updated passbook copy
  • Shows account holder name and address

Passport Size Photo

  • Recent color photograph
  • White background
  • 35mm x 45mm size (2 copies)

LC / Birth Certificate

  • Leaving Certificate or Birth Certificate
  • Proof of age and identity
  • Original required for verification

CERTIFICATE INFORMATION

Income Certificate

Issued to prove annual family income. Required for educational scholarships, fee waivers, and government schemes.

Domicile Certificate

Proof of residence in a particular state. Required for government jobs, college admissions, and property transactions.

Non-Creamy Layer

For OBC candidates whose family income is below ₹8 lakhs annually. Required for reservation benefits in jobs and education.

EWS Certificate

For economically weaker sections (general category) with family income below ₹8 lakhs annually. Provides 10% reservation.
