PAN CARD INFORMATION

The Permanent Account Number (PAN) is a 10-digit alphanumeric identifier issued by the Income Tax Department of India. It is essential for various financial transactions and tax-related activities.

Who Needs PAN?

• All taxpayers

• Business entities

• Foreign nationals conducting business in India

Key Uses

• Filing income tax returns

• Opening bank accounts

• Property transactions

• Securities investments

Important Notes

• PAN is mandatory for transactions over ₹50,000

• Only one PAN per individual is permitted

• Providing false information is punishable

Processing Time: New PAN applications typically take 15-20 working days for processing. Updates and corrections may take 10-15 working days.
